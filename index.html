HOW TO PLAY

You can PLAY VIA SMS, MPESA or WEB! Choice is yours to go for AUTOPICK, SELF PICK or PLAY NOW.

REGISTER

  1. SMS JOIN to 29XXX.
  2. You’ll receive a confirmation SMS to welcome you and confirm your account has been created.
  3. You’ll also receive deposit instructions e.g. Send (Amount) to Paybill 29XXXX

VIA SMS

  1. For AUTOPICK Send LOTTOCO to 29XXXX
  2. For SELF PICK SMS 1*2*3*4*5*6*0 (Pick your choice of numbers between 1- 49 & between 0-9).
  3. or PLAY NOW: AUTO PICK command will apply.

VIA M-PESA
